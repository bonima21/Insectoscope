About Us

Insectoscope is a AI insect Identification platform

 Insectoscope is a visionary project concentrating on the subject of identifying insect species. Our project addressed the need for reliable equipment for entomologists, researchers, or individuals interested in insects to cut the intensive labor and the user error that could occur when one wants to identify an insect.
